Let’s not beat around the bush – blogdown is a godsend for academics who want a nice website without putting down days’ worth of time on reinventing the wheel. In fact, this website only took around 4 hours to go from learning about blogdown and hugo to the final draft. The more difficult part, ironically, involved figuring out how to get the site live. The authors of blogdown: Creating Websites with R Markdown recommend using Netlify to simplify the workflow for publishing one’s own site. However, since I use workflowr extensively in my own work, and I would rather not have to juggle two hosting sites at once, I prefer to use GitHub Pages for hosting vazquez.bio.
